Game

"Trashy ocean hero"

A game played near water sources and/or beaches that teaches about creating a cleaner ocean with the used of dice!

What you need

Two six-sided dice

How to play the game

The goal in this game is to pick up trash from the ocean to keep our beaches and ocean trash free. You can play this with a group of friends, but if you're younger, make sure to have an adult always with you. You start by rolling the two dices, the person who got the least amount of the score added up by the two dices will have to do the challenge of picking up plastic. After you think you've picked up enough trashes, look at the table in the next section to see how many points you'll get for each type of item found. Every other players playing will otherwise get 2 point.

Table points:

Trash Points
cigarette / cigarette filters 1 points
food wrappers / containers 1 points
plastic / beverage bottles 1 points
bags 1 points
caps, lids 1 points
cups, plates, forks, knives, spoons 1 points
straws, stirrers 1 points
beverage cans 1 points
coin 2 points

Why play this game

Plastics are part of our daily life, but at the same time they are also a big part of our waste stream. Marine debris is a fact, either by floating in the water, washed up on our coasts or underwater. These marine debris results in big damages to the marine ecosystems. For instance, marine life can get trapped in plastic debris or being killed, as the fishes, turtles and other marine mammals often mistakenly consider them as food. To save the ocean water from trashes, means saving many lives. And that makes you a hero!
